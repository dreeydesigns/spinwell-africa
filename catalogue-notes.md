# Spinwell Catalogue Notes

This note captures key product information extracted from the viewed pages of `SpinwellCatalogueofBikes.pdf` so the website build can proceed without needing to re-open the same pages.

## Page 2 — Style NO.01 Adult blender smoothie bike

The adult blender smoothie bike is shown in multiple colours. The catalogue states that six colour options can be chosen from the set, and the visible colours include white, pink, green, orange, and royal blue. The wheel cover options shown are orange and watermelon.

The dimensions listed on the page are approximately **165 cm (L) × 70 cm (W) × 100 cm (H)**. The listed weight is **35 kg per set**, and the maximum load is **150 kg**. The carton size is shown as **106 × 94 × 34 cm**.

The whole set includes **1 bike**, **1 set plastic jar set (2 pcs)**, **1 basket**, **1 horn**, and **2 wheel covers**.

A note at the bottom of the page states that the whole set bike including the upgraded blender jar set is priced at **USD 1200 per set**, and the jars listed are **S1200 ml + L1800 ml = 2 pcs per set**. An extra moving wheel is available at an extra charge.

## Page 3 — Style NO.02 Kids smoothie blender bike

The kids smoothie blender bike is presented as a smaller version of the smoothie bike. The visible price information on the catalogue page states **USD 420.00 per set**, and a highlighted total for five sets states **USD 2100.00**.

The dimensions shown are approximately **135 cm (L) × 70 cm (W) × 95 cm (H)**. The weight is listed as **28 kg per set**, and the maximum load is **90 kg**. The carton size appears as **99 × 84 × 34 cm**.

The whole set includes **1 bike**, **1 set plastic jar set (2 pcs)**, **1 basket**, **1 horn**, and **2 wheel covers**.

The colour remarks indicate that the kids bike comes in combinations using **white bikes and pink bikes**, paired with **orange and watermelon wheel covers**. The page also repeats that the upgraded plastic blender jar set includes **S1200 ml + L1800 ml = 2 pcs per set**.

## Page 4 — Style NO.03 Adult pedal electric bubble bike

The adult pedal electric bubble bike is shown with a bubble machine attachment. The visible colour options again include multiple bike finishes, with the remark line specifically mentioning **white bike + orange and watermelon wheel cover** as one set example.

The dimensions listed are approximately **165 cm (L) × 70 cm (W) × 100 cm (H)**. The weight is shown as **38 kg per set**, and the maximum load is **150 kg**. The carton size appears as **106 × 94 × 34 cm**.

The whole set includes **1 bike**, **1 set bubble machine**, **1 set electric box**, **1 basket**, **1 phone holder**, and **1 set wheel cover**.

The page also notes that the **bubble machine without bubble water** is part of the offering, and the power specification shown is **output 24V, power inverter 400W**.
